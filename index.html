Kesher Nafshi Europe was established to bring the trusted guidance and support of Kesher Nafshi to communities across Europe.

Our work focuses on helping parents strengthen connection, rebuild understanding and respond with clarity, calmness and compassion.

No parent should feel alone when facing a sensitive family or chinuch challenge.
